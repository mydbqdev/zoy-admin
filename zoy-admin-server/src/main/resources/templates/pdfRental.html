LEAVE AND LICENSE RENT AGREEMENT

NOW THIS RENT AGREEMENT WITNESSETH AS UNDER:

That the Tenant shall pay rent on time to the Owner through ZOY Tenant App on or before the 5th day of the rent cycle of every month.

Owner has the right to send rent reminders through ZOY mobile app and WhatsApp, and payment receipts will only be issued digitally through the mobile app.

That the Tenant shall use the said premises only for residential purposes of self and not for any other purposes.

That the Tenant shall not sublet the said premises or any portion thereof to any person or persons under any circumstances.

One set of original keys of the said premises has been handed over to the Tenant, and in case any of the door keys are lost, the tenant will bear the cost of replacing it with a new lock, or else the amount will be deducted from the security deposit paid by the Tenant.

That the Owner will be at liberty to inspect the said premises at any reasonable time with prior intimation in the presence of the Tenant.

That the Tenant will maintain the said premises, and he/she shall always keep the same premises neat, clean, and tenantable worthy, and not damage the said premises or change any portion thereof in any circumstances.

In case this agreement is renewed for another period of extended months, the parties agree to execute a new agreement for the same as per mutually agreeable rent and terms.

That the agreement may be terminated earlier by either side giving a 30 days advance notice in writing to the other party. That in case of failure to pay rent for any particular month, it shall entitle the Owner to terminate this Agreement without serving any notice of 30 days as mentioned above.

That the Tenant will not violate any terms and conditions of the concerned Government authorities of Water/Electricity and all other concerned Government or local agencies relating to the above said premises.

The Tenant has no ownership rights whatsoever on the said property. Further, the Tenant undertakes to not encumber the said property in any way whatsoever. The Owner will not be responsible or liable for any action of the Tenant which may cause any disadvantage to the Owner or his/her property or property rights.

That all electricity, water, maintenance charges if any before this date are the responsibility of the Owner and hereinafter these charges will be borne by the Tenant.

Cost of damages if any to the said premises and/or any fittings and fixtures mentioned in clause 12 of this agreement shall be recovered by the Owner from the interest-free security deposit.

That both parties shall abide by the rules and regulations of Rent Control Act and terms and conditions of this agreement.

ANNEXURE 2 - Task to be done Via ZOY Platform

TENANT KYC –

As per tenancy model act, we are required to complete your Background Screening, Joining Form, Rent Agreement and Police Verification for safety and legal reasons. To make it easy for you, we have made

it completely digital. Therefore, you must enter your complete details, Govt ID and other required information through our check-in link before or within 24 hours of Date of Joining.

Your KYC data might be shared with our authorized technology and govt. partners to complete your Background Screening, E- KYC, Police Tenant Verification/Registration, and Rental Agreement.

Payments -

All dues such as Rent, Electricity, water, maintenance will be shown on real-time basis on our tenant app (powered by ZOY)

The TENANT will make payments only via the Exclusive Payment link shared on their WhatsApp numbers or via our Tenant App or our exclusive payment QR (powered by ZOY).

Our payment methods support all popular UPI apps including Paytm, PhonePe, GPay, BHIM, NEFT/ RTGS/ CREDIT CARDS/ DEBIT CARDS. There may be a payment/technology processing charge over the total due amount.

If the TENANT wants to pay in CASH, they can pay only via THE AUTHORIZED LIST OF RENT COLLECTORS FOR PG Name administration.

Immediately after the successful payment, the TENANT will be provided an automatic digital payment receipt on their WhatsApp/Email and tenant app. There will not be any paper receipt provided.

Food menu –

Every week's latest food menu and timings will be updated on our tenant app. You don't have to call or message anyone to know the food menu and timing.

If the TENANT comes after the meal timings have passed, it will be the tenant's responsibility to arrange food for oneself.

Announcements/Digital Notice Board 

All the relevant and important information, notice and announcements (such as electricity cuts, Water issues, Wi-Fi, Mess timings etc) will be sent to you on our tenant app (powered by ZOY). You will also be notified for the new announcements and messages via WhatsApp/SMS by ZOY.

Complaints –

On noticing any maintenance issues such as plumbing, wiring, etc. in your room or anywhere in the property, you are immediately requested to register an issue/complaint through our tenant app.

However, the TENANT must register only genuine complaints and not misuse it. If found registering fake complaints, there may be a fine of Rs 500.

The TENANT will get all real-time notifications on your complaints when a team member is assigned to the issue and when the issue is resolved.

Leave Property/Eviction Notice –

Any other mode of leave/eviction notices will not be accepted.

Late Check-in –

In case of late night entry curfew being applicable, the TENANT is required to register a "Late Check-in Request" by 9 pm.

The entry gates are closed by 10:00 PM, i.e., night curfew time at our property. In the absence of a Late Check-in request, the TENANT may not be allowed to enter.

Guest Invite –

In case of any guest visiting the premise, the TENANT must add all the necessary details of their guest in our tenant app for management's information and approval.

The entire process is digital for your convenience allowing instant entry and no more long waits and calls with guards.
